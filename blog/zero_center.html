The Importance of Zero-centering in ML

30 Jan 2023

What is Zero-centering? Firstly, it is a Linear Function.

For a given dataset with $k$ datapoints, with $m$ features, we do the following: $$(x_1,...,x_m)-(\bar{x_1},...,\bar{x_m})$$

Essentially, we subtract the mean from the data, resulting in the global mean of all the datapoints to be 0.
